 Digital Literacy Project
 Overview

This project focuses on understanding digital literacy and its importance in students’ academic and professional lives. It explores how students use digital tools, communicate online, build their digital presence, and stay safe in digital environments.

The project highlights that digital literacy is not just about using technology, but also about being responsible, ethical, and aware of risks such as cybercrime, miscommunication, and misuse of platforms.

 Objectives
To understand the concept of digital literacy
To analyze student behavior in digital spaces
To develop awareness about safe internet practices
To improve professional communication skills
To build a responsible digital presence
