Social Media Do’s and Don’ts
Do’s
● Maintain a professional and respectful tone in
your posts and comments
● Verify information before sharing to avoid
spreading misinformation
● Use privacy settings to protect your personal
data
● Share achievements, skills, and positive
activities to build a strong online presence
● Think before posting—consider how it may affect
your future opportunities
Don’ts
● Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate
content
● Avoid oversharing personal details like phone
number, address, or passwords
● Do not engage in online arguments or negative
discussions
● Avoid posting content that could harm your
reputation or career
● Do not blindly trust links, messages, or unknown
profiles (risk of scams)
