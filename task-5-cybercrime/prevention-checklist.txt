Stay Safe Online – Checklist  
Prevention Tips 

●​ Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable 
two-factor authentication (2FA) 

●​ Never click on suspicious links or download attachments from 
unknown sources 

●​ Always verify URLs before logging in—check for “https” and correct 
domain name 

●​ Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive transactions 
●​ Regularly update your apps and devices to fix security 

vulnerabilities 
●​ Do not share OTPs, passwords, or personal details—even if the 

request seems official 

 Financial / UPI Safety (Important) 
●​ Never approve unknown payment requests or “collect requests” on 

apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm 
●​ Double-check the receiver’s name before sending money and avoid 

scanning random QR codes 

 Additional Safety Habits 
●​ Log out from shared devices and avoid saving passwords on public 

systems 
●​ Be cautious while sharing personal information on social media 

 Reporting CybercrimeIf you face any cyber fraud or suspicious activity: 

Report immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: 
cybercrime.gov.in 



Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (for immediate financial 
fraud support)
